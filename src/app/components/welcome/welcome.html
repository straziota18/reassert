How to use this tool?

If you just started playing, close this page. Try it out and when you start having more than 10 recipes, 4 factory types and you realize "maybe I cannot determine everything I need to build on my own" you can come back


I recommend starting with the schedule tab and add some resource targets. You can then use the "Add missing factories" button to create the minimum amount of factories needed. Finally head to the inventory tab and check how many factories you need. When you get to the actual building, follow these rules:

  • Take as much space as possible - otherwise you will not have space for the rails
  • Add a storage depot after a production group. I see 2 benefits:
    • have a safety buffer when you have a demand burst
    • Allow for the possibility of large factories (you will see an endless stream of items on a rail - I find it oddly satisfying)
